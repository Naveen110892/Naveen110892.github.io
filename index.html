About Me

Hello! I am Naveen Kumar, currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at DGIST, South Korea. I have extensive research experience in the fields of energy harvesting, ferroelectricity, and piezoelectric materials. My work primarily focuses on the electromechanical properties of functional ceramics and their applications in energy harvesting and storage devices.
